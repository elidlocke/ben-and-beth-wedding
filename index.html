Our Story

Summer Love

We met in the summer of 2010. We were both out skateboarding, when a mutual friend introduced us. We made plans to meet up on the first day of summer. And we didn't stop hanging out for the rest of that summer. Or ever since.

Over the last 8 years, we've travelled together, moved across Canada, to Europe and back home. In 2016, we took a trip to Algonquin park to go cross country skiing and ice skating, where Ben proposed in front of a frozen waterfall.

We chose a summer camp to get married at because we both love being outdoors -- and because we're both super Canadian.

The Big Weekend

We just can't get enough of you, which is why we hope you'll spend the whole weekend with us!

  • Arrival & Check In

    Friday Sept 7th, Evening

    For those of you who can make it up on Friday evening, check in starts at 6pm and camp dinner is at 7pm. Bring your own beer and spend the night hanging out by the firepit with us.

  • Food & Camp Activities

    Saturday Sept 8, Morning

    Wake up, go for a swim in the lake, then join us at 10am for Breakfast. After Breakfast the camp staff will open exciting activities like rock climbing and canoeing. Of course you can also sleep in and join us for lunch at 1.

  • Ceremony

    Saturday Sept 8th at 4pm

    Throw on a nice shirt or sundress and head over to watch us tie the knot. Our wedding ceremony will start at 4pm sharp.

  • Reception

    Saturday Sept 7th at 6:30pm

    At 6:30pm, head up to the main lodge for dinner on us. Prepare for funny speeches, wedding games, scrumtious snacks, and bad dance moves.

  • Goodbye Hugs

    Sunday Sept 9th at 11am

    We don't want this weekend to ever be over. Unfortunately our check out time is 11am.

Where to stay

We strongly recommend that you stay with us at our venue, Camp Cedar Ridge. That way, you can dance 'till you drop then stumble back to your bed.

Camp Cedar Ridge

B.Y.O.Sleeping Bag. Shared cabins or rooms in the main lodge at the same location as our ceremony & reception. $100/person for both Friday and Saturday night or $50/person for just Saturday night. This accomodation option includes all meals and requires no driving all weekend.

Address: 57 Cemetery Rd, McArthurs Mills, ON K0L 2M0

Sword Inn Bancroft

For those who require more accessibility and/or comforts than the camp can provide, we reccomend the Sword Inn. The Sword Inn is a 30 minute drive away in Bancroft,ON. We did not reserve a room block here, so book early!

Address: 146 Hastings St N, Bancroft, ON K0L 1C0
